About us

AstroPrint


Welcome to AstroPrint! A sticker shop created by a group of ICT students from the Polytechnic University of the Philippines. We are a team of creative individuals who are passionate about design and self-expression.


Our shop offers a wide variety of stickers for all types of interests and personalities. From cute and quirky to cool and edgy, we have something for everyone. All of our stickers are made with high-quality materials and are designed to last. We believe that stickers are a fun and easy way to add personality to your belongings and express yourself. Whether it's decorating your laptop, water bottle, or even your car, our stickers are the perfect way to make a statement.


As students, we understand the importance of affordability, so we strive to offer our stickers at a reasonable price. We are constantly updating our inventory with new designs, so be sure to check back often!


Thank you for visiting AstroPrint, and we hope you enjoy our stickers as much as we enjoyed creating them.
